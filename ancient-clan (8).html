The Lore

Fragments recovered from the Ancient Clan archive. Three ages, one clan.

I. The Sundering

The old empire fell not to war, but to a fire that would not die. Its capital still burns beneath the ash-plain, and its embers call to those who remember.

II. The First Coal-Bearers

Seven survivors carried a shard of the eternal fire from the ruins. They founded the clan, and swore no ember would ever go out again.

III. The Ashen Rite

Every member who joins Ancient Clan passes through the Ashen Rite — a trial of memory, loyalty, and fire — before their sigil is struck.

IV. What Remains

Today the clan holds the last living ember of the old world. Its keepers watch, its ranks grow, and the fire has not gone out.
